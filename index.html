New Released Lego Sets Wishlist

Barad-dûr LEGO Set

Published: October 13, 2024

Author: Aidan Marentette

The Barad-dûr set is a highly anticipated LEGO set that brings the iconic tower from the Lord of the Rings universe to life. This detailed set includes intricate architecture and is a must-have for any fan of the series. Standing tall, the set showcases the dark and foreboding essence of Sauron's stronghold, perfect for display or imaginative play.

Jabba's Sail Barge LEGO Set

Published: October 13, 2024

Author: Aidan Marentette

Jabba's Sail Barge is another iconic LEGO set from the Star Wars universe. It recreates the famous scene from "Return of the Jedi," where Luke Skywalker faces off against Jabba the Hutt. The set includes intricate details of the sail barge, along with multiple minifigures like Jabba, Leia, and R2-D2. It's perfect for collectors and Star Wars fans who want to bring this legendary scene to life.
